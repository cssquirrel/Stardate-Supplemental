13: Holographic Cheesesteak

15 Jul 2018 • 78 minutes • Wiki Link

download episode as mp3

The Darwin Genetic Research Station is hell-bent on making Kahn 3.0 wearing a neon onesie. Is it the clashing colors that are distracting Starfleet from catching on? Doc Kingsley and her fellow ultra-racist eugenics BFFs are all now aging rapidly as their eerie adult-looking kids kill them with weird immune systems and intense eye contact. If that wasn't bad enough, the Brothers Weems have so many uncomfortable questions about holodecks and insertion. It's the episode with a record-breaking use of the word 'cheesesteak'. Check it out!
