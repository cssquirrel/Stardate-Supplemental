The Q'Maire is among the top of the line when it comes to Talarian warships... which means when it comes to a slugging match with the Enterprise it's rockets and x-rays have no chance of accomplishing anything. But Captain Endar is ready to fight for his son, Jono, even if he is just another random blonde heartthrob from the 90's. Also, the Brothers Weems are fiending hard for nachos. Like too hard. Like shredded targ and spicy pepper nacho hard. Melt that cheese and strap in, it's a new pod!
